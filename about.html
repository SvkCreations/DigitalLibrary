About Us

Fountainhead

Globally, all colleges and universities are built are built around its library as it is central point being considered as fountain head of education and knowledge. Named after Ayn Rand 1943 novel,The Fountainhead, the iLEAD main library nurtures reading habits of students and attracts book lovers across the World. Built on a sprawling area of over 3900 sq.ft and with almost 1,20,000 books, the library is literally a treasure trove of an ever growing collection of books, journals, magazines and newspapers.

Explorer World

Explorer World, the Audio Visual Library has an area of 800 sq.ft. It has over 2500 CDs/DVDs and more than 2,000 e-resources which include e-books, documentaries, tutorial and articles on various subjects. The digital library is eqipped with 12 MACs and 5 desktops for users. Both the libraries together have a sitting capacity of 124 students.
